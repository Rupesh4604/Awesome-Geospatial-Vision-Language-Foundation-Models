Prototype & evaluate

Smallest viable version. Vision: GEO-Bench-2 subset, linear probe + LoRA. VLM: LHRS-Bench + RSMMVP probes + grounding IoU, against a frontier generalist baseline.

Evaluation discipline: linear-probe before fine-tune · few-shot scaling at 100/1k/10k labels · cross-region hold-outs · DINOv3 baseline for vision, GPT-4o-class baseline for VLMs · report grounding IoU separately from text metrics · probe hallucination explicitly.
